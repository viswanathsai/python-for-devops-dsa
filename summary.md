# 16-Week Python + DSA for DevOps Summary

Each week = ~5 hours (1 hour × 5 days). Weekends are for rest or optional review.

## Phase 1 – Foundations (Weeks 1–4)
- Week 1: Python lists & basic loops
- Week 2: Strings & simple parsing
- Week 3: Arrays with two pointers
- Week 4: Sliding window technique

## Phase 2 – Fast Lookups (Weeks 5–7)
- Week 5: Dictionaries (hash maps)
- Week 6: Hash map patterns & frequency counting
- Week 7: Sets & deduplication

## Phase 3 – Ordered Processing (Weeks 8–10)
- Week 8: Stacks (LIFO)
- Week 9: Queues & deque (FIFO)
- Week 10: Sliding window revisited (with deque)

## Phase 4 – Recursion & Trees (Weeks 11–12)
- Week 11: Recursion fundamentals
- Week 12: Binary trees & basic traversals

## Phase 5 – DevOps Core Algorithms (Weeks 13–16)
- Week 13: Graph basics (BFS/DFS)
- Week 14: Topological sort (dependency resolution)
- Week 15: Heaps (priority queues)
- Week 16: Consolidation & interview-style practice
